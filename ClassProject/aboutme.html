Highlighted Trips

Trip To Africa

Modal Content For Reference

This picture was from a hot air balloon ride in Africa. I chose the picture because it best encapsulated my feelings about that trip as a whole. The trip mainly comprised of animal safaris as well as sightseeing on the open savannahs of Africa. Being able to see Lions, Giraffes and Wildebeests in their own natural habitat was surreal to see and is by far one of the best experiences I have ever had in my youth. The hot air balloon ride was especially special since it gave me the opportunity to see all of the animals from a birds-eye view with the sunrise in the distance. This is absolutely an experience that I will never forget.

Trip To Japan

This picture was taken when I was on a trip to Japan many years back. As you can see in the picture, I ended up going in the spring based on the Cherry Blossoms in bloom.
